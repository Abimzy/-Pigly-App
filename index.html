About Pigly

We are a group of piggybackers that loved giving and receiving a them so we decided to make it a business! We started this service in 2018 with helping an old lady across the street and to her home! Now we are out there going on adventures and making new connections and experiences with our new friends(that’s you!)

We care about making that new connection with you and being your buddy in your journey to your destination. We believe the best rides are shared with another and piggybacking is the closest you’ll get to another person. We’re also experienced in jumping over the pitfalls and over the rails while maintaining that joint venture to the goal. Those other rideshare services? They can’t build that one on one connection like we can also while having a 0 emissions policy! We get the workout while you get the comfort and unique views while we arrive to your stop! We are Pigly, the piggybacking rideshare service for that new experience you’ve always needed!
